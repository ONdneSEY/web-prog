What Is Precision Medicine?

Proactive, preventative and longevity-focused concierge medicine designed for you.

At the heart of precision medicine are metrics that monitor your health over time. The aim is to thoughtfully track your biology and lifestyle to support you in living your best life. This approach includes state-of-the-art diagnostics, relevant wearable technology and a comprehensive lifestyle audit to guide decisions that directly improve your healthspan.

The result is a personalized road map that gives you the confidence to maintain your health and freedom.

Choosing your concierge primary care practice is one of the most important decisions you can make about your future.

Understanding each practice’s philosophy and financial incentives is crucial when selecting a partner to help you reach your healthcare goals.

When interviewing a new practice or physician, ask them:

01

What type of relationship can I expect with my primary physician?

02

Does the practice mark up or profit from tests or prescriptions?

03

What is the relationship between physicians across your practice and network?
When and how do I get access to them as part of my care?
