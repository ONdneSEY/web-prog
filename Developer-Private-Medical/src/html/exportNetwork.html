Expert Network Spotlight

Eric Verdin, M.D.

President and CEO

The Buck Institute for Research on Aging

One of the world’s most highly cited scientists, Dr. Eric Verdin has led the Buck since 2016. His lab studies the relationship between aging and the immune system.

A native of Belgium, Dr. Verdin received his medical degree from the University of Liege and completed additional training at Harvard Medical School. He previously held positions at University of Brussels, National Institutes of Health, Picower Institute and Gladstone Institutes.
